The Journey of Taste

Our Heritage

A Legacy in the Making

Crafting Excellence, Preserving Tradition

Mantra Thakali Nepalgunj was established with a singular vision: to honor the timeless culinary traditions of the Himalayas while setting a new global standard for boutique hospitality.

Every Thali served within our walls is a meticulously curated experience. From the high-altitude jimbu of Mustang to the hand-milled spices, we bring the soul of the Thak Khola region to your table.

The Leadership Vision

A Message From Our Founder

"Mantra Thakali was never meant to be just another name in the industry; it was born from a relentless pursuit of perfection and a deep-rooted respect for our Himalayan heritage.
